GOAL
Explore 3D space, 3D collisions, animation, and basic enemy AI.

OVERVIEW
This project is a full 3D level, where the player battles an ancient robot. Finite state machines manage the player's actions, including sword attacks, movement, charging and firing a laser pistol, and dodging.

Spheres are used to detect character overlap with other colliders. To resolve collisions, the game calculates an appropriate resolution point and pushes the player out of the overlapped object.

The collision system limits the angle of slopes the player may traverse. Moreover, players "stick" to the ground when descending a slope. Special thanks to Erik Ross, whose character controller laid the groundwork for my collision resolution.

The robot's AI is controlled by a finite state machine and makes decisions based on player location. I introduced a random element to the AI's decisions to provide a sense of surprise every battle. Hitting the arms or chest damages the boss.

I created and animated all 3D models in Blender.
