Crystals and Curses
I am the designer and one of two programmers developing Crystals and Curses. Our team, Leap Doctor Games, believes that games have the ability to enrich people's lives. They are the perfect blend of visual art, story, sound design, problem-solving, and fun.

Crystals and Curses respects players' intelligence with challenging, ever changing puzzles. I hope you love it as much as we do. You can follow our development on twitter:
@LeapDoctorGames.
Players break curses by connecting crystals, turning them all white as they go from start to finish. As designer, I focused on progressing gameplay in the same way that story progresses. Players will continuously discover and utilize new mechanics as puzzles become more difficult and complex.
Crystals and Curses is more than a series of puzzles. It has fast-paced boss battles, gambling lobsters, riddles, inhabited suits of armor, and...secrets.
