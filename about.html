About

I'm a full-time student at the University of South Florida pursuing a BS in Computer Science. My team at Leap Doctor Games is currently developing a puzzle game - Crystals and Curses. Follow us here.

I've always been drawn to video games as they are the crossroads of multiple art forms like story, music, sound, animation, and visual art. Games allow us to slay dragons, dog-fight in space, and challenge our minds with complex puzzles. They tell stories of life and death, mistakes, love, and friendship.

Outside of class, I have invested my free time creating games and other software, some of which I have included here.

I hope you enjoy!
