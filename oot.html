THE TREE OF OOT

GOAL
Build a simple, complete game from scratch.

OVERVIEW
My first game, The Tree of Oot, is a 2D endless-dodger, where the player controls Oot, a flying squirrel. Oot can open his wings to slow his fall and better dodge pinecones. Each successfully dodged pinecone increases the player's score.
